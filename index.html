About me

Innovation, thinking out of the box, finding solutions for the things difficult to think about are the things that interests and excites me

I'm pursuing my B.tech in Computer Science from NIIT University. Belonging to a middle class family whose members from generations opened their own shops and earned their living, I wanted to stand out and do something out of my knowledge and skills.

Throughout the years of my engineering I looked for different skills, challenges and new things to learn every day.

During my first year, I got introduced to AIESEC which is the world's largest youth run organisations formed after the events of WW 2. This really excited me and gave me a sense of adventure. I worked for the projects that were handled and soon promoted to "INTERNATIONAL GLOBAL VOLUNTEER HEAD". After 3 successful exchange programs and welcoming 26 International Students to our university, within 6 months I got promoted to "PRESIDENT of AIESEC in NU" and sent 40+ NU students to International Intersnhips.

I learned Graphic Desiging via youtube and did several internships and freelancing projects. Dealing with different people in the office and working for different people gave me a basic knowledge about how things work in offices and what it means to work for 8 hours straight.

In the beginning of my second year I started Web development and Angular 4. Side by side started my own venture with the name "Nailon" for the local barbers, developed an E-commerce from scratch. Later did internship in Angular Web Development. Where I faced challenges like coding down a major part in a single day and learned to work in a developer team. Also, I was shown how to handle clients which indeed helped in my own venture.

At the end of second year, I started Android Development and made few working applications like Pedometer, Augmented Reality applications.
